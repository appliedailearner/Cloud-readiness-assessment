# Audit Report: CRA Template Content Review
**Date:** February 12, 2026
**Panel:** Senior Strategy Panel (Exec, Architect, CISO, IT Ops) & Target User Group

---

## 1. Executive Strategy Panel (Score: 4.2/5.0)
*“The templates provide a clear 'Decision Bridge' between data and strategy.”*

### Inputs/Critique:
- **Project Plan:** Excellent inclusion of the 11-week timeline. Needs a dedicated "Executive Milestone" row for SteerCo sign-offs.
- **TCO Model:** Powerful simplification. High-value callout on the "Migration Bubble" (Double Run costs) is a differentiator.
- **Exec Presentation:** The sequence is logical. Suggest adding a "Risk of Inaction" slide to drive urgency.

---

## 2. Cloud Architecture Panel (Score: 4.5/5.0)
*“Technically sound, especially the automated discovery emphasis.”*

### Inputs/Critique:
- **Evaluation Matrix:** The weighting scale (1-10) is industry standard. Suggest adding "Data Sovereignty" as a mandatory P1 criteria.
- **Infrastructure Discovery Report:** Identifying "Zombie Servers" is a huge win for TCO. Needs a section for "Platform Modernization Candidates" (IaaS to PaaS).
- **Dependency Map:** Ensure the template encourages tagging by "Application Affinity" rather than just IP subnets.

---

## 3. CISO & Security Review (Score: 4.0/5.0)
*“Good baseline, but needs sharper teeth on Compliance.”*

### Inputs/Critique:
- **Communication Plan:** Requires a "Security & Data Privacy" workstream lead specifically for the Discovery Phase.
- **Cloud Strategy Report:** Needs 1-2 pages dedicated to "Shared Responsibility Model" education for the board.
- **Gap Analysis:** Should mandate the use of the **Zero Trust Maturity Model** as the benchmark for target-state security.

---

## 4. IT Operations & Delivery (Score: 4.7/5.0)
*“I can actually use these to start Week 1.”*

### Inputs/Critique:
- **TCO Inputs:** Very practical. Including "Labor Efficiency Factor" shows real-world experience.
- **Asset Inventory:** The "Week 0" requirement is critical. Without it, the project stalls.
- **Project Plan:** Suggest adding a row for "Tooling Permission Procurement" which is the #1 delay in real projects.

---

## 5. End User / Blog Reader (Score: 3.8/5.0)
*“Helpful, but show me the 'How'.”*

### Inputs/Critique:
- **Usability:** Templates are clean. Users want to see a **downloadable ZIP** of all 7 files instead of raw Markdown.
- **Context:** Brief "Instructional Comments" inside the templates (e.g., "Replace TBD with [X]") would improve speed of adoption.

---

## Final Recommendation:
**APPROVED WITH ENHANCEMENTS.** The templates are "Principal-Grade" but would benefit from a final layer of "How-To" annotations for first-time assessors.
